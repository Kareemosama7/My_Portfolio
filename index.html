01 — About

The Art Behind The Numbers

I'm Kareem Osama — a Performance Marketer and self-taught systems builder with a Computer Science background.

I'm also a guitarist, and that's not a random detail — it shapes how I approach campaigns. Music is structure and feeling at the same time: technique that only means something once it moves someone. I bring the same instinct to performance marketing. Every campaign I build has to hit two things at once — the art of the creative (the hook, the story, the thing that actually makes someone stop scrolling) and the discipline of the system underneath it (tracking, testing structure, budget logic) that turns that moment into a measurable result.

My CS background is what makes the second half possible — I don't just run ads, I build the infrastructure behind them: testing frameworks, tracking systems, and budget logic that scale without breaking. The numbers are the proof. The craft is what gets you there.
